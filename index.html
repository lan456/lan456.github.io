Short Bios

    Yan Meng is a 5th year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China. He is also a visiting scholar in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Virginia. He received his B.S. degree in Electronic and Information Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2016. His research interests include wireless network security and privacy.
